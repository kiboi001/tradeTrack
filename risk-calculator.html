💡 Risk Management Rules

🎯

The 1% Rule

Never risk more than 1-2% of your account on a single trade. This alone can save you from blowing your account during a losing streak.

📐

Minimum 1:2 RR

Always aim for at least a 1:2 risk-to-reward ratio. With a 40% win rate and 1:2 RR, you're still profitable.

📉

Max Drawdown Guard

Set a daily loss limit (e.g. 3%). Once hit, stop trading for the day. Emotions compound losses faster than you think.

🧮

Position Sizing Math

Lot = (Balance × Risk%) ÷ (SL pips × Pip Value). Always calculate BEFORE entering — never guess your size.

🔥

After a Losing Streak

After 3 consecutive losses, consider halving your position size until you recover your confidence and read the market correctly again.

✅

Breakeven Win Rate

Formula: 1 ÷ (1 + RR Ratio). At 1:2 RR, you only need to win 33% of trades to break even. Know your number.
