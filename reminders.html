📋 Core Trading Rules to Remember

These are universal principles that separate disciplined professionals from emotional traders.

  • 🛡️ Protect capital first. Your goal is to KEEP your money, then grow it. Survival comes before profit.
  • 📐 Never risk more than 2% per trade. A losing streak of 10 trades should not end your account.
  • ⏳ Wait for your setup. Not trading IS a position. Missing a trade is far better than taking a bad one.
  • 🚫 No revenge trading. After a loss, step away for at least 30 minutes before reviewing the next setup.
  • 📓 Log every trade. You cannot improve what you cannot measure. Even losses teach you something.
  • 🎯 Stick to your take profit. Moving TP further out of greed is how good setups turn into losers.
  • 🧘 Trade the plan, not emotions. If you feel excited or anxious about pressing the button, pause and review.

🎯 Weekly Trading Goals

Set clear, achievable weekly goals. Focus on process — not just profit. Good process leads to consistent profit.

  • 📅 Log every trade taken this week in the journal, including screenshots where possible.
  • 🔍 Review any losing trades — identify if the loss was due to bad process or just market randomness.
  • 💡 Identify one pattern that is working well this week and note it in your trade journal.
  • 📉 If you hit 3 consecutive losses in a day, stop trading for that day — no exceptions.
  • ✨ End the week with a brief summary of what you learned. Continuous improvement beats short-term wins.
