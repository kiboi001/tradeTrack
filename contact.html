❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for a subscription? +
Go to the Plans page and click "Get Pro Monthly" or "Save with Annual". You'll be redirected to Paystack's secure checkout page where you can pay via M-Pesa or card. Once payment is confirmed, your subscription activates automatically.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore), encrypted at rest and in transit. We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure only you can access your trade journal data. Payments are handled entirely by Paystack — we never see your card or M-Pesa credentials.
What happens when my subscription expires? +
You get a 3-day grace period after expiry where your premium features remain accessible. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan — but your data is never deleted. Simply renew to regain full access.
Does the app work on iPhone and iPad? +
Yes! TradeTrackFX is fully optimized for iOS Safari on both iPhone and iPad. If you experience any loading issues, please clear your browser cache and try again. You can also add the app to your home screen for a native app-like experience.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Subscriptions are manual (not recurring auto-billing). You won't be charged again unless you manually purchase another cycle. Just don't renew when it expires and you'll naturally return to the free plan.
